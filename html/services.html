Our services

Road Assistance

At My Ride, we understand that unexpected events can happen while you're on the road. That's why we offer a comprehensive road assistance service to our customers, providing you with peace of mind and ensuring that you can enjoy your journey with confidence. Our road assistance service is available 24/7, with quick response times and trained, certified service providers who are ready to help you with a wide range of emergencies, from flat tires and battery jump-starts to lockouts and fuel delivery. We believe in transparent policies and costs, so you can rest assured that you won't be hit with any hidden fees or charges. Our multi-channel communication options, including phone, email, and online chat support, make it easy to reach us when you need us. We proactively monitor the status of each request for assistance, providing you with regular updates and estimated arrival times, so you always know what to expect. At My Ride, we're committed to providing you with the best possible rental car experience, and our road assistance service is just one of the ways we demonstrate our commitment to your safety and satisfaction.

Extra driver

At My Ride, we understand that long road trips can be tiring, which is why we offer an extra driver service to make your journey more comfortable and enjoyable. With our extra driver service, you can switch drivers as needed, allowing you to take breaks and share the driving responsibilities with a friend or family member. Our extra driver service is easy to add to your rental agreement, and our fees are affordable, so you won't have to break the bank to enjoy the convenience of an extra driver. All of our drivers are screened and approved by us, so you can rest assured that your safety is our top priority. We believe that driving should be a pleasant experience, and our extra driver service is just one of the ways we help make that possible. With My Ride, you can sit back, relax, and enjoy the journey, knowing that you have a trusted partner by your side.

FAQ

Q: What types of vehicles can I rent from your rental system?
A: Our rental system offers a wide range of vehicles, including Bikes, Tuks, Cars and Vans. We have different sizes and models available to suit your specific needs.



Q: What is the minimum age requirement for renting a vehicle from your rental system?
A: The minimum age requirement for renting a vehicle is 21 years old


Q: What do I need to rent a vehicle from your rental system?
A: You will need a valid driver's license, a credit card, and proof of insurance. Some rental locations may require additional documentation or identification, so please check with our customer service team before booking.



Q: How do I make a reservation for a rental vehicle?
A: You can make a reservation online through our website, or by calling our customer service team. Simply choose your rental location, dates, and the type of vehicle you need, and we'll take care of the rest.



Q: What is your cancellation policy?
A: Our cancellation policy varies depending on the type of reservation and rental location. Please check your rental agreement or contact our customer service team for more information.



Q: What happens if I get into an accident with the rental vehicle?
A: If you are involved in an accident with the rental vehicle, please contact our customer service team immediately. We will provide you with instructions on how to proceed, including filing an accident report and arranging for a replacement vehicle if necessary



Q: How do I return the rental vehicle?
A: You can return the rental vehicle to the same location where you picked it up, or to a different location if one-way rental is allowed. Please make sure to follow our return instructions, including fuel level, cleanliness, and any damages.
